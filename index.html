Rules:

  • Each player is dealt 13 cards
  • Human starts first
  • Lead with any card except Spades
  • Win the play by playing the highest card of the suit led
  • Ace is highest, 2 is lowest
  • Spades win any trick except when only Spades are played, then the highest Spade wins
  • Spades can only be used if you don't have cards that match the current play or only have Spades in your hand
  • Each trick won is worth 10 points
  • Who ever wins the most points wins the game
